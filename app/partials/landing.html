HIPAA-compliant Video Conferencing

  • Call scheduling and management made easy.
  • Privacy compliant with national security standards.
  • Designed for patients and doctors, used by anyone.

In war-torn countries, the ratio of doctors to patients at refugee camps is approximately 1 to 2060. More than 200,000 civilians in Syrian camps have died from non-communicable chronic diseases. The telemedicine application provides a low-cost, sustainable solution to the physician shortage crisis by allowing doctors who are unable to travel or stay at remote and under-resourced countries for long periods of time to communicate and follow-up with patients, obtain patient clinical information, make an accurate diagnosis, and develop a treatment plan in person and over video-call. Cornell Engineering World Health is currently working alongside Speetar, a startup founded by the physicians at Massachusetts General Hospital, to develop a prototype, and thus hippo was born.
